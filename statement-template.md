# Model Influence Statement

## Instructions

This statement is a voluntary transparency document for authors who want to disclose whether and how machine-learning models were used in the creation of a research work. The listed authors are responsible for the accuracy of the disclosure and for verifying that any AI-generated code or text used in the work performs as intended.

By sharing this information, authors can support clearer reporting practices and help build a longer-term culture of reproducible, responsible model use. Over time, these disclosures may also help model creators, reviewers, and research communities better understand how models are used in practice and how disclosure standards should evolve.

Reviewers are encouraged to value the transparency reflected in this statement while evaluating the work on its own merits, and not to treat the presence, absence, or level of detail in the disclosure as a standalone signal of research quality.
